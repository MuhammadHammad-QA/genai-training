**Detailed Summary of Emumba's Leave Policy**

### 1. Purpose of the Policy
The purpose of Emumba's leave policy is to ensure fairness, transparency, and balance between work and personal life. The policy aims to support employees' well-being and provide them with the time they need to rest, recharge, and attend to personal matters.

### 2. Employee Eligibility
The leave policy applies to all individuals working in a full-time capacity at Emumba.

### 3. Leave Types
The company offers various types of leave, including:
* Paid Time Off (PTO): 15 working days per year for vacation, personal time, and recharge.
* Sick Leave: 5 working days per year for personal illness or caregiving for loved ones.
* Maternity Leave: 60 working days for normal birth, 70 working days for premature birth or medical complications, and 10-20 working days for pregnancy loss.
* Paternity Leave: 5 working days for supporting a newborn and family, and 10 working days for premature birth or medical complications.
* Special Occasion Leave: 10 working days (once every 2 years) for own marriage or religious pilgrimage (Hajj/Umrah).

### 4. Leave Entitlement
The leave entitlements are as follows:
* PTO: 15 working days per year
* Sick Leave: 5 working days per year
* Maternity Leave: 60-70 working days (depending on the circumstances)
* Paternity Leave: 5-10 working days (depending on the circumstances)
* Special Occasion Leave: 10 working days (once every 2 years)

### 5. Approval Process
The approval process for leave requests involves:
* Using BambooHR's Time Off Request Module
* Approval from the employee's manager
* Informing the team about the leave
* Following up with HR if the leave is not approved within 48 hours

### 6. Carry-Forward Rules
The policy does not explicitly state carry-forward rules, but it mentions that the combined total of Special Occasion Leave and PTO must not exceed 15 days.

### 7. Special Conditions
The policy provides special conditions for:
* Pregnancy loss: up to 20 working days of paid leave
* Premature birth or medical complications: additional leave days for maternity and paternity leave
* Flexible working hours and remote work options for new parents
* Access to a wellness coach and licensed counselor for emotional support

### 8. Restrictions and Exceptions
The policy has the following restrictions and exceptions:
* Leaves cannot be taken during the notice period
* Leaves require approval from the manager and HR
* Clients must be informed about the leave, especially in augmented teams
* Assuming leave can be taken if the manager is away is not allowed
* Disciplinary actions and escalation steps may be taken for leave misuse

Overall, Emumba's leave policy aims to provide a supportive and flexible work environment that allows employees to balance their work and personal life.
